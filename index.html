Messianic Tech

The Messianic Tech Slack is a space for people in the Messianic community who are in tech to chat and support each other. We strive to keep it safe, positive, and confidential.

How do I join?

The Slack is invitation-only, but invitations are freely granted. You can request one from any existing member or by filling out this form:

Who’s invited?

Any person who believes in Yeshua, is Jewish or identifies as part of the Messianic community, and works or is interested in tech. This includes designers, developers, project managers, IT workers, web masters, etc... This includes those working in congregations, organizations, companies, and hobbyists

Channels

Slack chat spaces are divided into rooms called “channels”, and we have a few. There are two you’ll be in by default:

  • #general: occasional announcements
  • #random: random chat

All rooms are opt-in except general, which is why it is kept low-volume.

What are the rules?

We have a simple and uncontroversial Code of Conduct.

TL;DR: be nice, and what happens inside generally stays there unless someone specifically says otherwise.

If you're under 18, please let the admins know so we can keep an eye out for you.
